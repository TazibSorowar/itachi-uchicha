Who is ITACHI ?

Itachi Uchiha was a shinobi of Konoha's Uchiha clan who served as an Anbu Captain. Who later became an international criminal after murdering his entire clan, sparing only his younger brother, Sasuke. Afterwards joined the international criminal organization known as Akatsuki.

Why Did ITACHI Eliminate His Whole Clan?

Itachi Uchiha was ordered by the elders of Conoha to kill his clan in order to prevent a civil war in the village. Itachi simply followed the orders he was given and caused the extinction of his own clan. He didn't spare his own parents nor the women he loved. Only his little brother Sasuke was spared.
